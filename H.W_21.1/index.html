Yorkshire Terrier

The Yorkshire Terrier is a small dog breed that I really like. Despite its small size, this dog is very active, brave, and friendly.

Yorkshire Terriers are known for their beautiful long coat and lively character. They are very loyal to their owners and enjoy being close to people.

This breed is intelligent and easy to train. Yorkshire Terriers are often playful and curious, which makes them great companions.

Yorkshire Terriers are perfect for home living and bring a lot of joy and positive emotions.
